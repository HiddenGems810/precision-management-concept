# Phase 2A: High-Fidelity Art Direction System

Before executing final assets, Phase 2A will explore three distinct visual directions within the "Enterprise property management / Operational trust" lane. We will strictly avoid futuristic SaaS clichés, luxury condo branding, and AI-generated imagery, focusing on scalable communication infrastructure and warm professionalism.

## 1. Concept Explorations (3 Directions)

For each direction, we will establish:
- **Logo Concept:** Primary mark, secondary mark, and icon.
- **Landing Page Visual Direction:** Hero styling and color balancing.
- **Print Collateral Direction:** Business card and one-sheet vibe.
- **Owner Acquisition Campaign Direction:** Data-driven ad/email template style.
- **Resident Communication System Direction:** Service standards card and email styling.

### Direction A: The Institutional Standard
- **Vibe:** Highly structured, authoritative, data-first.
- **Color Emphasis:** Precision Navy and Concrete Gray with minimal accents.
- **Typography:** Rigid geometric sans-serif (Inter) mixed with a highly traditional serif (Literata) for authority.
- **Imagery:** Focus on clean architectural lines, structural integrity, and professional personnel.

### Direction B: The Operational Warmth
- **Vibe:** Approachable professionalism, service-first, reliable.
- **Color Emphasis:** Slate Blue and Paper White with Managed Green for highlights.
- **Typography:** Softer sans-serif with generous letter spacing to convey openness and transparency.
- **Imagery:** Focus on human interaction, property managers on-site, and well-lit, maintained environments.

### Direction C: The Modern Civic
- **Vibe:** Clear, definitive, highly practical, civic-minded reliability.
- **Color Emphasis:** High contrast Precision Navy against Paper White, utilizing Maintenance Gold for primary actions.
- **Typography:** Bold sans-serif headers with highly legible, utilitarian body copy.
- **Imagery:** Focus on infrastructure, scale (aerials or wide shots of multifamily properties), and crisp operational charts.

## 2. Core System Rules (Applied to all directions)

- **Typography Scale:** Strict semantic heading hierarchy (H1-H6) designed for web and print legibility.
- **Spacing System:** 4pt/8pt baseline grid for consistent structural rhythm across all assets.
- **Card/Component System:** Modular "Trust Cards" for metrics, and standardized forms for owner/resident touchpoints.
- **Motion Direction:** Minimal. Subtle fades and slide-ups only. No erratic or bouncy animations.
- **Image Treatment Rules:** Authentic color grading. No random gradients or duotones. Photography must feature real architectural context and professional service scenarios.
- **Mockup Style Rules:** Flat, isometric, or clean studio lighting. No overly complex environmental mockups that distract from the system design.
